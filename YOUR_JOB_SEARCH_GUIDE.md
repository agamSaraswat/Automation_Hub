```

[Paragraph 1: Why I'm interested in THIS company]
- Specific signal that caught my eye (product, research, market)
- Personal connection if any ("I built something similar")
- Show you've done homework

[Paragraph 2: Why I'm relevant for THIS role]
- 1–2 key accomplishments matching their need
- Technical proof points (shipped X, led Y)
- Show you understand their stage

[Paragraph 3: Call to action]
- Suggest 30-min call
- Ask to share my GitHub/work samples
- Express genuine interest

Soumyabrata
```

### Customization Tips

**For Pre-seed:**
- Emphasize: Speed, scrappy mentality, wearing many hats
- Mention: How you'd help with first hires
- Show: Revenue/impact you've driven

**For Seed/Series A:**
- Emphasize: Scaling, process building, team leadership
- Mention: How you'd handle 10→50 person growth
- Show: Technical depth OR product strategy

**For CTO Roles:**
- Emphasize: System design, infrastructure thinking
- Mention: Technical hiring experience
- Show: GitHub, system architecture decisions

### Don't Send Generic Emails

Generic template = Delete.

Specific mention of their product/mission = Higher response rate.

Example:
- ❌ "I'm interested in your startup"
- ✅ "Your approach to retrieval-augmented generation aligns with how I architected data layers at ReadlePress"

   - Run: `python run.py --jobs`
   - 5–10 jobs discovered via Claude semantic search

2. **Evaluate** (manual, 10 min)
   - Review discovered jobs
   - Read company website / LinkedIn
   - Score: Company culture + Role fit

3. **Tailor** (automatic)

   - Evaluator checks truthfulness

4. **Apply** (manual, with gate)
   - Direct apply on company site (best)

   - Record: Via, resume used, date

   - Dashboard shows: Pending, Responded, Interviews, Offers
   - ROI metrics: Cost-per-response, cost-per-interview
   - Feedback loop: Adjust keywords if response rate < 5%

**Quantity:**
- Target: 10–15 applications per week
- Timeframe: 2–3 month sprint

**Quality:**
- Response rate target: 10–20% (industry avg ~5%)

**Efficiency:**
- Cost-per-interview: < $5 (ROI measure)
- Time-to-response: < 7 days (good sign)

## Warnings & Gotchas

### Don't Fall Into These Traps

**Trap 1: "We're the next Anthropic"**
- Red flag: Every pitch says this
- Check: Actual revenue / users / differentiation

**Trap 2: "Equity will make you rich"**
- Reality: 0.1% at Series A is worth ~$15–30K (if acquired)
- Better: Good base + reasonable equity + 18+ month runway

**Trap 3: "You'll learn so much"**
- Translation: Underpaid + no mentorship
- Ask: Who will you learn from? How?

**Trap 4: "We're hiring fast"**
- May mean: No process, chaos scaling
- Check: Is there a senior tech leadership (not just 1 founder)?

**Trap 5: Visa Commitment Bait-and-Switch**
- If they say "we'll sponsor visa": Get it in writing
- Verify: Their history of sponsorships

### Deal Breakers

- No equity mentioned
- Equity < 0.3%
- Salary < $120k
- No remote option + no visa sponsor
- Founder with high turnover in previous teams
- Vague about funded amount or runway
